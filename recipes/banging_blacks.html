Banging Blacks

Description:

Blacks is an intense beachbreak with even more intense nudies. It breaks well on most swells. Summer is slower and Fall / Winter are when the magic happens. Don't forget to bring your crocs and hackey sacks.

Ingredients:

  • West swell direction (275 degrees)
  • Mid to long period (15 seconds ideal)
  • Low tide for barrels, high tide for ripping
  • Light offshore or dead wind

Steps:

  1. Park at glider port
  2. Argue with homeless
  3. Cocks and crocs - avert your eyes
  4. Find a cave
