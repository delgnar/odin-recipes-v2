Scrumptuous Swamis

Description:

Swamis: AKA the King of the Coast. A Proper Pointbreak, that comes with a disgusting crowd of logs. Get lucky and you might just run into Benny. Breaks best at night.

Ingredients:

  • WNW swell direction
  • Long period (15-100 seconds)
  • Low tide

Steps:

  1. Get a parking spot
  2. Paddle out if you dare
  3. Get frustrated and paddle over to the secret spot to the north...
